New UUID versions proposed in
draft-peabody-dispatch-new-uuid-format-04
as an update to RFC 4122

Version 6 - Field compatible with UUID1,
time fields in "correct" order

1ed34ea6 - 74a7 - 63dc - 9b24 - c957a35d0f7b
thththththththth - tmtmtmtm - vtltltl - xchchclcl - nnnnnnnnnnnn

th - time_high - The most significant 32 bits

tm - time_mid - the middle 16 bits

tl - time_low - The 12 least significant bits

v - version

v and tl are labeled together as time_low_and_version

th tm tl - 60 bits in total, count of 100-nanosecond intervals since 00:00:00.00, 15 October 1582

x - variant

ch - clock_seq_hi - Clock sequence can be incremented to generate UUIDs faster than the timestamp ticks

cl - clock_seq_low

n - node - IEEE 802 MAC address

Version 7 - Unix timestamp

0183411f - f81b - 7f55 - b958 - 166595f041f8
tstststststststs - tstststs - vrarara - xrbrbrbrb - rbrbrbrbrbrbrbrbrbrbrbrb

ts - Unix epoch timestamp, 48 bit big-endian unsigned number

ra - 12 bits pseudo-random data

rb - 62 bits of pseudo-random data
